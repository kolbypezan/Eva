About Me

Hi, I'm Eva Sage Ramos! I'm a passionate and dedicated animal lover with over three years of professional experience in dog walking and sitting. As a proud dog owner myself and a student pursuing a degree in Education, I understand the importance of patience, reliability, and creating a positive environment for every animal I care for.

Why Choose Me?

My commitment to animal welfare goes beyond just a job—it’s a core part of who I am. I believe every dog deserves to be safe, happy, and loved, especially when their family is away. I am prepared for any situation and take the responsibility of caring for your furry family member very seriously. Their happiness and safety are always my top priorities.
