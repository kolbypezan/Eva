About Me

Hi, I'm Eva Sage Ramos! I'm a passionate and dedicated animal lover with over three years of professional experience in dog walking and sitting. As a proud dog owner myself and a student pursuing a degree in Education, I understand the importance of patience, reliability, and creating a positive environment for every animal I care for.

Why Choose Me?

I have over 3 years going on 4 of dog sitting and walking experience and have two dogs of my own. I am fully prepared for any emergency or interesting circumstance that may arise with your fur baby. Also, fun fact: Im vegetarian, hoping to be vegan eventually. But why does that matter? It matters because it provides proof of my love for EVERY animal, not just the cute and furry ones. I am committed to fighting for animal (as well as human) rights and I take my job as a dogsitter very seriously. Their happiness AND safety is of utmost importance.
